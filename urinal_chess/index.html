Urinal Chess

Rules: Two players take turns occupying urinals with an ndless supply of men who have to pee. You can only occupy one urinal per turn, and if you can you must (everyone has to pee). Click a urinal to fill it. Complete your turn by clicking PC move. You win by filling the last available urinal.
You move first, or PC moves first if you click PC move.
